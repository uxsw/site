User Experience events in the South West

This is a new idea and looking for your feedback.

What is UXSW?

It's almost true to say - I don't know yet.

Still here? - OK, here's the background: in July I ran a small UX workshop in Exeter, and it got some really positive feedback (read the write up).

Following the day, I've heard from people who attended saying they'd like to do more and from people who didn't come saying they'd be interested if I ran anything else in future. There's already a number of UX focused events around the UK, but they seem lacking in the South West. UXBristol is our closest (and the event that finally prompted me to do this).

I'm not expecting this to be a full scale conference with 100+ attendees (at least not yet) - but I am trying to get a general gauge of interest.

Currently I'm thinking about a date in October, possibly in Exeter (mostly because I live there so it's easy for me) - with a small charge to cover costs.

Beyond that ... I don't know yet.

If you're interested, sign up for the newsletter and follow @UXsouthwest to keep up with progress.

What I'd really like from you is feedback on what you'd like. A day of related workshops? Talks? A combination of both? UX is a broad field - so what areas are you particularly interested in?

And of course - if you've got ideas for a talk or workshop you'd like to run - let me know.

Oh - and also feedback on if a week day or weekend is preferable.

So, it's all wonderfully vague at the moment, but I know the interest is out there so please let me know your opinions so I can try and ensure it's going to be something that is as valuable as possible for people.

Send your feedback, ideas and suggestions to john@uxsw.co.uk or @UXsouthwest
& please sign up for the newsletter to be kept posted as this gets more organised.
