Alan Colville

Engaging clients meaningfully in the process of digital design

Great digital experience happen when we engage clients, not just users, meaningfully in the process of digital design. This workshop describes techniques, which not only demonstrate the value of UX, but build better client / designer relationships.

Over the past decade or so, we've brought clearly into focus the people who use our digital designs.

Yet as the web has grown up, and with it UX design, clients are expected to keep pace with our ever changing industry, when even we struggle.

Now feels like a good time to pause, and focus on the clients who pay for our work, and consider how to better engage them in what we do?

Case studying existing and emerging techniques, this workshop will show how clients and designers are required to inhabit new and shared ground.

Done right, these techniques improve collaboration, build trust, and help deliver great digital experiences.

What attendees will learn?
How focusing less on 'tad-dah’, flat images, process and deliverables and more on the inevitable outcome, which happens when the client is engaged in the process of design, creates trust and delivers better digital experiences for everyone. Techniques for engagement with clients will include:

  • Vision development
  • Empathy mapping / personas
  • Experience mapping
  • Sketch boards
  • Collaborative sketching
  • Content strategy
  • Content reference diagrams
  • Style guides
  • Prototyping

Attendees will leave with a framework for engaging clients in the process of design. In addition, they will be eager to try what they have learned with their clients.

Alan Colville

colville.cx @alancolville

I’m a Customer Experience Consultant, speaker and author, living in Bristol, UK. I help companies create profitable experiences, that customers love.

I’ve spent more than a decade building and leading teams in a variety of roles; as the customer experience guy for large companies like BT and Virgin Media, UX chap for design agencies, a designer in a web start-up, and more recently as the owner at Colville CX.

Colville CX is a Customer Experience Consultancy, where we combine research, strategy, user experience & design to create digital experiences that are profitable for companies, and loved by customers. We know that great customer experiences don't just happen. They are based on vision and strategy, meticulously planning, innovative design and unwavering commitment to bring to life.

Previously, I spoke at UX Bristol and at the Telecoms IQ’s 5th Customer Experience Management in Telecoms Global Summit in London. I’ve published on UX Magazine, 52 Weeks of UX, and UX Booth. I’ve also had the pleasure of featuring in Simple and Usable by Giles Colborne.

Originally from Tipperary, Ireland, I live with three women, two of whom are my daughters, making me significantly outnumbered on a daily basis. These days, you might catch a glimpse of me running up mountains or biking down them; most recently at The Andalucia Bike Race, where with other deranged mountain bikers we went up and downhill for 6 days.
